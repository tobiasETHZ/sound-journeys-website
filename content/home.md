## Introduction to the concept

### Kicker
The Concept

### Section title
Something between a concert and a shared inner experience

### Body — paragraph 1
A Sound Journey is a curated listening experience — typically 90 to 120 minutes of continuous, carefully sequenced music played through a high-quality system in an intimate space. You rest, listen, and let the arc carry you.

### Body — paragraph 2
The experience is structured with intention: a grounding phase, a deepening, peaks of intensity, release, and a slow return. Every journey has a different emotional character, a different shape.

### "Not this / not that" line
Not a concert. Not therapy. Not a guided meditation.
A curated, inward-facing group experience with a strong emotional arc — artistic rather than therapeutic, structured rather than ambient.

## Anatomy

### Kicker
Anatomy of a Sound Journey

### Section title
Attention → Intensity → Release

### Phase 1 — Arrival
**Symbol:** ○
Settling in. Orientation. Letting go of what's outside. The music begins gently, inviting a softening of attention.

### Phase 2 — Deepening
**Symbol:** ◐
Texture builds. Layers emerge. Immersion takes hold. Tension and melody begin their conversation.

### Phase 3 — Waves & Peaks
**Symbol:** ●
The arc doesn't simply rise — it surges, recedes, and surges again. Multiple crests. Emotional turbulence. Intensity finds its apex.

### Phase 4 — Release
**Symbol:** ◌
Spaciousness returns. The body softens. Silence arrives. A gentle re-entry — integration without instruction.

## What to expect

### Kicker
Entering the Journey

### Section title
What to Expect

### Intro line
There's no right way to experience a Sound Journey. Here's what helps to know beforehand.

### Item 1 — Posture
**Icon:** 🛋
**Heading:** You'll be lying down or resting
**Body:** Mats and cushions are provided. The experience is physically still — your attention does the travelling.

### Item 2 — Duration
**Icon:** ⏱
**Heading:** 90–120 minutes of continuous listening
**Body:** The full experience runs 2 to 3 hours including arrival and a gentle integration period afterwards.

### Item 3 — Emotional intensity
**Icon:** 🌊
**Heading:** Emotional intensity is possible
**Body:** Some journeys move through surprising emotional territory. This is a natural part of deep listening. You're always free to sit up, step out, or simply observe.

### Item 4 — Autonomy
**Icon:** 🤝
**Heading:** Autonomy stays with you
**Body:** There's no performance expected. Engage lightly or fully — both are welcome. Your boundary is your own.

### Item 5 — Phones
**Icon:** 📵
**Heading:** Phones stay away
**Body:** We ask that screens are off or put aside. The room works best when attention is shared and undivided.

### Item 6 — Container ethics
**Icon:** 🫧
**Heading:** A held container, not a guided one
**Body:** There are no instructions during the journey. The music and the arc do the guiding. We hold the space — you hold yourself.
